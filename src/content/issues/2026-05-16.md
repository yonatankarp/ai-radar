This is the seed issue for AI Radar. The production pipeline will replace this with curated daily briefings from AI Signal Desk.

## Editorial contract

Each issue should answer three questions:

1. What changed?
2. Why does it matter?
3. Who should care?

The public site should receive only curated, public-safe output. Raw ingestion data, private notes, Gmail/calendar context, and internal OpenClaw logs stay outside this repository.
